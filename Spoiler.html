Movie Quote

College Boy: [Licking back window of police car] The snozzberries taste like snozzberries

Plot

A group of highway patrol officers are in danger of losing their jobs from recent police budget cuts. Throughout the show they try to solve a large scandal/murder case to show their worth and not get cut. In the end they solve the case and expose deep police corruption but are still cut. They end up joining their rival police station.
